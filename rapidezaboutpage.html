The Rapid-EZ Approach is a rapid, novel method that uniquely balances and realigns inflamed soft tissue planes of resting limbs that have drifted off-axis due to strains or sprains.

As a result, the REZ Approach has repeatedly been shown to quickly diminish discomfort, swelling, stiffness, and apprehension, thereby, accelerating the restoration of function, performance, and resiliency in most cases within a week.

The highly proprietary REZ Approach employs a precision-engineered wearable tech design to harmonically weave a stabilizer around a resting limb using a series of updating algorithmic protocols customized to each individual and their injury.


And although the Rapid-EZ Approach does not correct structural damage requiring definitive readjustment--such as fractures or ligamentous disruptions--there is almost always some level of surrounding soft tissue inflammation that can be mitigated to accelerate reconditioning and restoration following injury.
